What this is And How To Us It

This tool is simple. You input how much you made in 2016, what if any property tax you paid, and estimated sales tax. Using this data and publicly available government data, we calculate exactly how much money you paid to various government programs and agencies. If you still unsure what any of this is, just try it out for yourself and see what you get!
